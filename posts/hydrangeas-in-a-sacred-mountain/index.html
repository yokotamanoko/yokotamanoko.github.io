Hydrangeas in a Sacred Mountain

I saw a lot of beautiful hydrangeas in the sacred place Mt.Yoshino.
Every clusters were so big and filled with energy. I felt this was reality. My eyes opened. I’d like to keep it in my mind.

In this year, I’m interested in reading postmodern books more.
Especially literature, philosophy and criticism spark my curiosity. Active authors provide me with inspiration and guidance. I receive divine afflatus through them.

For instance…

鏡の中を数える (Counting the Inside of a Mirror) by Prabda Yoon
http://www.typhoonbooksjapan.com/e/catalogue/kagaminonaka.html

ゲンロン (Genron) by Hiroki Azuma(ed.), et al
https://genron-tomonokai.com/genron/en/

These books teach me a possible next step in the future. The step is not too emotional. And it’s not too logical. It’s cool as a cucumber and so kind.

I like things I can feel a ray of sunshine.
All experiences are very good openers to open my eyes.
