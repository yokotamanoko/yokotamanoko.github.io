Awareness in Mt.Hiei

The first time I went to Mt.Hiei. The purpose of this time was greeting Saicho-san and Ganzan-daishi-san.

First of all, I visited Jodo-in.



After chanting a Buddhist sutra, I was wrapped in the silence.
I became a bird.
I became trees or flowers.
I became the sound of water dripping from 水琴窟(Suikinkutsu).
Everything was I, I was Everything.
I’d like to build on this experience to create my art as a tool of the sky.

After visiting Jodo-in, I walked towards Shaka-do and Ganzan-daishi-do.

This is Shaka-do.

This route is used in 千日回峰行(One Thousand Day Circumambulation).

This is 玉体杉(Gyokutaisugi).

The view(Kyoto) from the above tree was great.

I forgot to take photos of Ganzan-daishi-do…

After greeting Ganzan-daishi-san, I walked back the way and went to Konpon-chu-do. I dedicated my 写経(Shakyo) there.
I’d like to visit here next year again.
