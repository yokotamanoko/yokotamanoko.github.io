About

YOKOTAMANOKO

Watercolor Artist, IT Engineer

I was born in 1987, Japan. I started drawing and programming when I was a child. Since graduating from an university, I’ve been developing backbone systems of local governments and companies in the IT industry. Currently I’m not only working as an engineer, but also producing watercolors as an artist.

Deity, Buddha and Radiance

My life theme is “Radiance”.

There are many difficulties in this world. I’d like to approach the problems by creating arts. The main category is Watercolour. You can browse my work in the past on Instagram.
